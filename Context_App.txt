--- PROJECT SUMMARY ---

Construction workforce management platform (Kanban orders, scheduling,
equipment exchange, cost estimation) with dual-bot architecture (TG + MAX),
React frontend, FastAPI backend, SQLite database.

Stack: FastAPI + SQLite (WAL) | React + Vite + Tailwind | aiogram (TG) + maxapi (MAX)
Graph: 745 nodes, 783 edges, 109 communities (graphify)
